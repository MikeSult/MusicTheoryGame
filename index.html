Music Theory Game


Game Instructions

Use the arrow keys (⬅️,⬆️,⬇️,➡️) to move the butterfly. When you land on the musical notes you will be asked a music question. You will be frozen in place until you submit a correct answer. Eventually your correct answers will earn you 'applause' as you find a path to the globe in the lower right corner to refresh the game. Each row contains questions on a specific topic. Rows 1-3 deal with Note Identification. Row 4 is about Major Scales. Rows 5 and 6 are about Note values and Meter and Row 7 is on Major Key Signatures. Rows 8-9 are about Minor Scales, Rows 10-11 are about Intervals, Rows 12-13 are about Triads and finally the bottom row, Row 14, is about Modes of the major scale.
